My research (explained to an non-expert audience)

As a translational researcher I focus on bringing scientific discoveries back to the patients and also receive research ideas from my clinical work. I dedicate myself to the research and treatment of neurodegenerative diseases. These are diseases in which neurons progressively lose their function and die. Typical representatives of these diseases are Alzheimer's disease, Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis and Parkinson's disease. I evaluate biomarkers; these are tools that allow us to detect these diseases earlier (which is necessary to apply preventions and early therapies). Furthermore I am interested in bringing digitalization into the medical world in a way that is best for patients and healthcare workers.
