About me

I am a clinical neurologist at the University Hospital Bonn and an affiliated research associate at the German Center for Neurodegenerative Diseases (DZNE). In both research and in clinical practice, I focus on the discovery of biomarkers and explanation of pathological patterns through mathematical models in neurodegenerative diseases (primarily LBD, AD, and ALS/FTD). Being a translational neuroscientist, I create a link between problems that are brought up by clinicians and answers given by basic scientists. Furthermore, I strongly believe in freedom of information (not only) in research. Therefore, dedicate myself to bringing principles of open science to the clinical research community (to make it more reproducible, robust and inclusive).
